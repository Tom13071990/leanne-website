Speech & Myofunctional Therapy with Leanne Almog, SLP

I provide speech therapy and myofunctional therapy for children and adults, focusing on breathing, oral habits, speech clarity, and long-term functional health. My approach is personalized, evidence-based, and supportive for both children and parents.

My Vision

I help children breathe better, speak clearly, and feel confident in their bodies. Through personalized, compassionate myofunctional therapy, I support healthier breathing, communication, and long-term development—helping children unlock their full potential and build habits that last.

Through personalized, compassionate therapy, I support children and adults in communicating with confidence, breathing with ease, and living healthier, more empowered lives.

Our Areas Of Focus

Why Choose Me

Choosing the right therapist matters. I take a whole-child, root-cause approach that focuses on the habits behind breathing, eating, speech, and long-term health. I believe children progress best when they understand the purpose of therapy, feel respected, and enjoy the process. By working closely with parents and other professionals, and emphasizing consistency and collaboration, I help create meaningful changes that last.
