Speech & Myofunctional Therapy with Leanne Almog, SLP

Certified Speech-Language Pathologist & Myofunctional Therapist

I provide speech therapy and myofunctional therapy for children, focusing on breathing, oral habits, speech clarity, and long-term functional health. My approach is personalized, evidence-based, and supportive for both children and parents.

My Vision

I help children breathe better, speak clearly, and feel confident in their bodies. Through personalized, compassionate myofunctional therapy, I support healthier breathing, communication, and long-erm development- helping children unlock their full potential and build habits that last.

My Areas Of Focus

Better Breathing

Improving tongue position and oral habits helps children learn to breathe through their nose instead of their mouth. Better breathing often leads to improved sleep, more energy, and better focus during the day.

Better Eating

Enhance sensory processing, bilateral integration, attention, handwriting, and feeding abilities.

Better Speaking

Strong, well-coordinated mouth muscles help children produce clearer speech sounds. Supporting correct tongue placement and movement helps children speak more clearly and with greater confidence.

Why Choose Me

Choosing the right therapist matters. I take a whole-child, root-cause approach that focuses on the habits behind breathing, eating, speech, and long-term health. I believe children progress best when they understand the purpose of therapy, feel respected, and enjoy the process. By working closely with parents and other professionals, and emphasizing consistency and collaboration, I help create meaningful changes that last.
