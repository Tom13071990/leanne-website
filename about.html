Leanne Almog

I am a certified Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP), qualified Orofacial Myologist, and kinesiotaping practitioner with over 10 years of experience working with children and adults. My work focuses on improving speech clarity, oral function, breathing patterns, and long-term functional health.

Warm, personalized therapy is provided in English, Hebrew, and American Sign Language, tailoring each treatment plan to the individual needs of the child or adult. My approach is evidence-based, compassionate, and centered on building confidence through understanding and collaboration.

I work closely with families and other healthcare professionals to ensure therapy goals are meaningful, achievable, and sustainable over time.
